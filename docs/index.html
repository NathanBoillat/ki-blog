Künstliche Intelligenz

Our game is a rpg game based on the pokemon game series and the associated game princips. Overall our rpg game is devided into three different parts which are all presented by a specific island and asscociated fights. The youtuber Chris Courses was thereby our inspiration and he provided us with some usable sprites and ideas.

Funktionsweise

All of the three islands, which represent the whole game world, are suffering from a dangerous slime invasion. Save and rescue the citiziens from the slimes by fighting against them whith your own white pet slime, Alex! You begin the game in your own villige and move on to the other islands by boat. You are going to have to interact with different citiziens who provide you with helpful commentaries and hints. Every time you beat slimes, you gain experience and rise in level what influences the battle strength of your slime, Alex. The enemy slimes get stronger from island to island, which are reachable by boat, so first you have to beat the slimes of the first island and rise in experience and level up to be able to move on and fight the enemy slimes of the next islands. The whole population of The Three Islands are counting on you, so hurry up and good Luck!

Das Neuoronale Netz

First, good sprites and sources for the map and the player had to be found. These sprites were then imported to the map editor named Tiled. With this tool our map could be created by layering our sprites up to a specific layer, which all togeter form a map. Step by step the map was enlarged and details were added to it. Very important were also the collisions and the map boundaries, which already had to be placed in the map editor. Furthermore the foreground layers had to be fixed, so that the player could walk behind trees ect. what made the game more realistic.

Der Einfluss von Daten auf eine KI

Our game is an rpg game map. To move on the map and win, use the keys; W to move upwards, S to move downwards, A to move left, and D to move right. Within the fight scene, everything is automatic and strictly regulated. By pressing an attack button, Alex executes one of his currently learned attacks and damages the opponent. The opponent then attacks with a variable attack of his choice. This game principle is repeated until one of the two fighting Slimes has no more life points and the fight is over.
